Article VIII(E): "street or public way"

The last sentence states "No lot shall ever be used for or dedicated as a street or other public way to areas outside of Lakemoor Division 2 through 5." The project contemplated here is not a street or public way. "Public way" generally refers to passageways such as streets, alleys or sidewalks open to the public as a matter of right — contrasted to a "private way," defined as a way laid out by a private owner and maintained at their expense.

Swanson Law Firm, 2017

Bylaws

The Bylaws grant broad powers to the Directors of Lakemoor. Improvement of a walking path on land owned by the Club is clearly within the scope of the Board's powers under Paragraph 4.1.1 of the Bylaws.

This shows that acquiring an easement or walking path is within scope.
