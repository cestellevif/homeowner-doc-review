Articles IV & V — Park Area & Ken Lake Watershed Covenants

Article IV — Park Area Covenants

Clause IV.A — Dedication for Park Purposes

The park and community beach areas identified in Exhibit B are dedicated exclusively for park and recreational purposes, for the use and enjoyment of all Lakemoor lot owners and their successors.

Article V — Ken Lake Watershed Protection Area Covenants

Clause V.A — Watershed Protection

The Ken Lake Watershed Protection Area identified in Exhibit C shall be protected and used exclusively as the watershed for Ken Lake, for the benefit of all Lakemoor lot owners and their successors.

Committee Notes

Fix needed Article V is a declaration without substance — it dedicates the watershed area but imposes no specific protections or prohibitions. The actual watershed protections are scattered across Art. VIII. The revision should either consolidate watershed protections here or add an explicit cross-reference to Art. VIII.B and VIII.D so the protection framework is findable from the asset declaration.
